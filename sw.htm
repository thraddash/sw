Dagobah

You have just crash landed into the swampy marshlands of this tropical planet. Your X-Wing is quickly sinking into the thick bog and clammering out of the cockpit, you gently wade towards the shore intently aware that someone is watching.

You make it ashore, a bit injured from the crash landing but nothing you haven't experienced before. A huge shadow rustles past some bushes overhead and instinctively you pull out your lightsaber / laser pistol.
